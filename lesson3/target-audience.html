Target Audience:

The target audience for this site is women who work at home for their families and women who work outside. Especially for women, there are many things that depend on the weather every day.

Tow Personas

Amy

Job: Piano teacher

Age: 35 years old

Married with no child

Character: Outgoing and loves shopping and eating. Expecting to have some activities on the weekend.

Mary

Job: House wife

Age: 42 years old

Mother of 2 children

Character: Kind, quiet, loves cooking and baking. Expecting to have fun time with her family.
